About

With over 8 years of dance experience and 4 years of teaching experience, Yanni is a Singapore-based Hip Hop dancer, choreographer, and instructor, as well as a member of XEN. She sees dance as a conversation between music and the soul. With groove and flow at the core of her movement, she embraces a simple and honest approach to dance — allowing the music to guide her instincts, emotions, and expression in every moment.
